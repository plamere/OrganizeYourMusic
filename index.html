Organize Your Music

Organize your Spotify music collection by any of a wide range of musical attributes including genre, mood, decade of release and more.

Organize your music

Get your collection in order

With Organize Your Music, taking control of your saved music is simple. Just follow these steps:

1

Select

Choose what music to organize: Saved Music, added playlists, followed playlists, or everything.

2

Login

Authenticate with Spotify. We'll automatically sort your tracks into bins like Genre, Mood, and Decade.

3

Explore

Pick a bin. View track properties, plot the tracks visually, and listen to 30-second audio previews.

4

Stage

Select specific tracks you want to keep. They will automatically move to your Staging Playlist.

5

Save to Spotify

Review your staging playlist. When you are perfectly happy with the selection, explicitly save the new playlist directly to your Spotify account.

Your library is safe Organize Your Music will never modify or delete any of the songs in your saved music or existing playlists. It only creates and saves entirely new playlists for you.

The Track Properties

Slice and dice your collection by a wide range of powerful audio features.

  • Genre The primary genre classification of the track.
  • Year The release year of the recording.
  • Added The earliest date you saved this track.
  • BPM The tempo of the song in beats per minute.
  • Energy Measure of intensity and activity.
  • Danceability How suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Loudness The overall loudness of a track in decibels.
  • Valence Measure of musical positiveness (happiness).
